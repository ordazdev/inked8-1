Privacy Policy — Inked 8

Effective date: [08/27/2025]

Who we are

Inked 8 ("Inked 8," "we," "our," "us") provides AI-powered software and services for businesses (the "Services"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you visit our sites, use our apps, and participate in our SMS/MMS messaging programs.

1) Information we collect

Identifiers & contact data: name, email, phone number, company, billing details.

Commercial & usage data: account activity, pages viewed, logs, device/browser info, IP, cookies.

Content you provide: forms, support tickets, files you upload.

Communications data: your preferences and message content (email, chat, SMS/MMS) where applicable.

Integrations: data from tools you connect (e.g., CRM, calendar)—subject to your permissions.

2) How we use information

Provide, secure, and improve the Services; personalize features; analytics.

Messaging (email, chat, SMS/MMS): send account notices, authentication, service alerts, and marketing where you've consented.

Compliance with law, fraud prevention, and to protect our rights. (Initial and recurring disclosures and unsubscribe instructions are required by A2P/CTIA guidance.)

3) Mobile/SMS Program & Privacy

If you opt in to receive text messages from Inked 8:

Program description: automated and/or manual texts about account updates, reminders, product notifications, and promotions (message content as described at the opt-in point).

Consent: By providing your mobile number and opting in, you authorize Inked 8 to send SMS/MMS to that number using an autodialer or similar technology. Consent is not a condition of purchase. (Include this at opt-in.)

Frequency & fees: Message frequency varies. Message & data rates may apply.

Opt-out: Text STOP to cancel at any time. You'll receive a one-time opt-out confirmation text. Text HELP for help. You may also contact us at support@inked8.com or [+1 (XXX) XXX-XXXX].

No mobile sharing for marketing: We do not sell or share mobile numbers with third parties or affiliates for their own marketing or promotional purposes. We may disclose numbers to vendors strictly to provide messaging services (e.g., carriers, platform providers) and as required by law.

Carrier disclosure: Delivery is subject to your carrier's network; carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

4) When we share information

  • Service providers/Processors supporting hosting, analytics, messaging, payments.
  • Legal & safety requirements.
  • Business transfers (e.g., merger or acquisition).

We do not share SMS consent or mobile numbers with third parties/affiliates for marketing.

5) Cookies & analytics

We use cookies and similar technologies for core functionality, preferences, and analytics. Manage preferences via your browser or our banner.

6) Data retention

We retain personal data for as long as needed for the purposes above, to meet legal obligations, and to resolve disputes.

7) Security

We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

8) Your rights

Depending on your location (e.g., GDPR/UK GDPR/CCPA/CPRA), you may have rights to access, delete, correct, or port your data, limit processing, and opt out of certain uses. To exercise rights, contact privacy@inked8.com

9) Children

Our Services are not directed to children under 13 (or older, as required by local law). We do not knowingly collect such data.

We may update this policy. We'll post the new date and, if material, notify you through the Services or by email.
